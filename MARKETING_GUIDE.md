## ğŸ“Š Tracking & Analytics

### Key Metrics to Monitor

| Metric | Target | What It Means |
|--------|--------|---------------|
| CTR (Click-Through Rate) | >2% | Ad is engaging |
| CPC (Cost Per Click) | <$1 | Efficient ad spend |
| CPA (Cost Per Acquisition) | <$5 | Profitable |
| Conversion Rate | >3% | Landing page works |
| ROAS | >2:1 | Making money |

### Facebook Pixel Events to Track
1. **PageView** - When someone visits
2. **ViewContent** - When they see the landing page
3. **AddToCart** - When they click "Buy"
4. **InitiateCheckout** - When they start payment
5. **Purchase** - When they complete payment ($10 value)

### Google Analytics Goals
Set up these goals in GA:
1. **Goal 1:** Clicked Buy Button
2. **Goal 2:** Started Checkout
3. **Goal 3:** Completed Purchase (Value: $10)
